About Doshi Impex

This high jewelry necklace, crafted by Doshi Impex in 2014, is a sublime work of art that embodies the grandeur of ancient craftsmanship and timeless elegance. Designed for an exclusive client, the necklace is a masterpiece featuring meticulously selected Polki diamonds—uncut diamonds that radiate with a raw, natural beauty—alongside rich emeralds (Panna stones) and fiery red rubies. The central medallion is a striking representation of traditional Indian iconography, showcasing a deity figure, intricately hand-carved, emanating power and grace. Framed by rows of luminous rubies and diamonds, the jewel evokes a harmonious balance between divine symbolism and opulent luxury. The necklace’s rustic yet sophisticated finish reflects the extraordinary hand craftsmanship of artisans from Jaipur and Navsari—two revered hubs of Indian jewelry-making. One group of artisans painstakingly set the uncut diamonds, while the other worked on the detailed assembly of the precious metals and gemstones. The result is a bold and authentic statement piece, merging heritage with high jewelry sophistication. This heirloom-worthy creation encapsulates a deep respect for tradition, craftsmanship, and luxury, with each element hand-selected and assembled to create a piece of eternal beauty.

Celestial Halo

This stunning necklace, crafted in 2012 for an exclusive client, epitomizes the essence of regal luxury and artistry. The piece is an opulent masterpiece, made of pure gold and encrusted with meticulously set Polki diamonds—uncut gems known for their raw and natural allure. Each diamond sparkles like the stars of a royal court, casting a soft brilliance that is both ethereal and timeless. The necklace's design is intricately layered, with a mesmerizing central motif that radiates outwards in a sunburst pattern, symbolizing both grandeur and sophistication. Dangling from the lower edge of the piece are lustrous drops of pearls, which sway gently with every movement, adding a sense of fluidity and grace to the otherwise stately design. The pearls, in their soft elegance, contrast beautifully with the structured geometry of the Polki diamonds, evoking a sense of both softness and strength. The delicate balance of traditional craftsmanship with contemporary elegance makes this necklace a quintessential representation of royal luxury. Its luminous Polki diamonds, complemented by the timeless purity of pearls, make it not just a jewel, but an heirloom to be treasured for generations.

Eternal Brilliance

This exquisite pendant and earrings set, crafted in 2003, transcends the ordinary, capturing the essence of divine brilliance and luxury. At the heart of the design lies a trio of majestic rose-cut diamonds, each one shimmering with the ethereal brilliance akin to a god’s watchful eye. The unique facets of the rose cut radiate a soft, enchanting glow, creating an interplay of light that draws the gaze inward to the stone’s mysterious depths. Encircling these celestial diamonds are meticulously set round diamonds, each selected for their perfect symmetry and fire. These brilliant diamonds serve to magnify the splendor of the center stones, creating a halo effect that evokes both grace and grandeur. The contrast between the romantic allure of the rose cuts and the crisp sparkle of the surrounding diamonds creates a harmonious balance. The pendant, suspended on a delicate, yet resilient gold chain, reflects the timeless sophistication of the design. Each earring mirrors the elegance of the pendant, forming a radiant trio that speaks to the wearer’s appreciation for both heritage and refinement. Crafted with precision and care in 2003, this set is more than jewelry—it is a symbol of eternal beauty, artfully named "Celestial Halo." This timeless creation embodies elegance for those who seek to wear art close to their hearts.
